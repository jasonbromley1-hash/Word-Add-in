Getting Started

The Estate Clause Helper allows you to quickly search and insert common legal clauses into your Word documents.

  1. Click the "Open Clause Helper" button in the Home ribbon.
  2. Use the search box to find relevant clauses.
  3. Click "Insert into Document" to add the clause at your cursor position.
